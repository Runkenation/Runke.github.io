Who are we?

We (runke) are a 3 person alternative rock band. The three of us have grown up with a strong friendship, a deep connection to music theory, and a strong desire to make music.

Runke is, simply put, a project by and for people who love music. We in Runke all grew up loving genres like Punk Rock, 1st-2nd wave Emo, Indie, and Shoegaze.

The group roles are as follows: Dane on guitar, Deytan on drums, and Lucas on bass.

We love music, and we hope you'll join our little tribe and enjoy music with us. We love you dude, chill...
