Who are we?

We (runke) are a 3 person alternative rock band. The three of us have grown up with a strong friendship, a deep connection to music theory, and a strong desire to make music.

Runke is a Alabama based band

We in Runke all grew up loving genres like Punk Rock, 1st-2nd wave Emo, Indie, and Shoegaze.

The group roles are as follows: Dane on guitar, Nick on drums, and Lucas on bass.
